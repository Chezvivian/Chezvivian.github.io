      <h3 style="margin-top:0; color: #222;">知识点汇总</h3>
      <div style="color: #444; line-height: 1.6;">
        <h4 style="margin-top:0; margin-bottom: 1em; color: #222;">Key Points of Managing Innovation (7th Ed.) / 《创新管理》(第7版)关键点</h4>
        
        <div style="margin-bottom: 1.2em;">
          <strong>1. Social entrepreneurs</strong> differ from business entrepreneurs in motivation, timeframe, and resource utilization, possessing high empathy and sense of social justice.<br>
          <strong>社会企业家</strong>在动机、时间框架和资源利用方式上与商业企业家不同，具有高度同理心和社会正义感。
        </div>
        
        <div style="margin-bottom: 1.2em;">
          <strong>2. Social innovation</strong> often emerges from urgent needs combined with resource constraints, addressing problems unresolved by traditional public and third-sector organizations.<br>
          <strong>社会创新</strong>常源于紧迫需求与资源限制的结合，解决传统公共和第三部门组织未能解决的问题。
        </div>
        
        <div style="margin-bottom: 1.2em;">
          <strong>3. Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)</strong> has become a core function in many businesses, with stakeholder engagement helping to avoid conflicts and open new markets.<br>
          <strong>企业社会责任(CSR)</strong>已成为许多企业的核心职能，通过利益相关者参与可以避免冲突并开拓新市场。
        </div>
        
        <div style="margin-bottom: 1.2em;">
          <strong>4. Social innovations</strong> serve as learning laboratories, with innovations from extreme conditions potentially spreading to broader populations.<br>
          社会创新作为<strong>学习实验室</strong>，在极端条件下产生的创新可能后续扩散到更广泛人群。
        </div>
        
        <div style="margin-bottom: 1.2em;">
          <strong>5. Inclusive innovation</strong> focuses on the 4 billion people at the "bottom of the pyramid" by reimagining business models to meet low-income market needs.<br>
          <strong>包容性创新</strong>关注"金字塔底部"的40亿人口，通过重新构思商业模式来满足低收入市场需求。
        </div>
        
        <div style="margin-bottom: 1.2em;">
          <strong>6. Humanitarian innovation</strong> creates social value under crisis conditions through diverse actor networks, including product and process innovations.<br>
          <strong>人道主义创新</strong>在危机条件下通过多样化行动者网络创造社会价值，包括产品创新和流程创新。
        </div>
        
        <div style="margin-bottom: 1.2em;">
          <strong>7. Sustainability-Led Innovation (SLI)</strong> evolves through three stages: operational optimization, organizational transformation, and system building.<br>
          <strong>可持续发展导向的创新(SLI)</strong>通过三个阶段演进：运营优化、组织转型和系统构建。
        </div>
        
        <div style="margin-bottom: 1.2em;">
          <strong>8. Responsible innovation</strong> requires incorporating sustainability and broader social impact considerations into the innovation process to avoid unintended negative consequences.<br>
          <strong>负责任的创新</strong>要求在创新过程中纳入可持续性和更广泛的社会影响考量，避免产生意外负面后果。
        </div>
      </div>
